THE PATH'S BEST RELAXATION RETREAT

The Path, is a place of unprecedented serenity, privacy and comfort, purpose-built for VIPs and honeymooners. Just 10 north-facing luxury suites – The Path's largest – boasting 270-degree-wide views of the island, with stunning sunset and sunrise views.

The Path’s luxury suites – all of which feature private pools – are dotted around an awe-inspiring infinity pool and invite you to craft your own story in an unrivaled setting.

Our Head Butler is here to ensure you won’t lift a finger during your stay, while The Path's most influential Chef invites you to an exclusive gastronomic journey under the stars. Meanwhile, our on-site Althea Boutique Spa offers a holistic wellness experience only for two.

The Path was named World’s and Europe’s Best Honeymoon Hideaway at World Boutique Hotel Awards 2017 and World’s and Europe’s Best Relaxation Retreat at World Boutique Hotel Awards 2018.

GIORGOS kopatsaris
Engineer & Entrepreneur

The Path is the legacy project and brainchild of the late Giorgos Kopatsaris, the renowned engineer who since 1982 and until 2011, led most of the hotel development in Santorini. The Path gave him the opportunity to develop a property with virtually no compromise on quality and features, purpose-built for the needs of the VIP luxury traveller.

Today his design values and unwavering commitment to quality remain more relevant than ever, while his unique way of life inspires to never settle for less but to seize the day and work hard towards a truly lasting impact.
